About This Wood

The Story of Silver Oak

Silver Oak (Grevillea robusta), also known as Southern Silky Oak, is a striking hardwood native to eastern Australia, now widely cultivated across tropical and subtropical regions including South India. It was originally introduced to Tamil Nadu as a shade tree for tea and coffee plantations.

What makes Silver Oak truly distinctive is its beautiful lace-like grain pattern — a result of ray-flecked medullary rays that create a shimmering silver effect on quartersawn surfaces. This unique visual character makes it highly sought after for decorative and furniture work.

At Majestic Timber Depot, Silver Oak is one of our most popular offerings for customers looking for a beautiful, workable timber at an accessible price point. It is widely used across Tamil Nadu for furniture, interior panels, and plywood manufacturing.

Did You Know?

Silver Oak is one of the most widely planted trees in Tamil Nadu's Nilgiri hills — originally planted to shade tea plantations, it is now a major commercial timber species across South India.

Applications

Best Uses of Silver Oak

Furniture
Tables, chairs, wardrobes, and beds — Silver Oak's workability and attractive grain make it a popular choice for quality home furniture at an accessible price.
Decorative Panels
Wall panelling and feature panels — the distinctive lace-like ray pattern creates stunning decorative surfaces that add character to any interior space.
Plywood Core & Face
Widely used in plywood manufacturing — its straight grain and workability make it ideal as both a core material and a decorative face veneer.
Interior Joinery
Door frames, window linings, skirting boards, and mouldings — Silver Oak's easy workability produces clean, precise results for interior finishing work.
Cabinetry
Kitchen cabinets, storage units, and display shelving — Silver Oak finishes beautifully and holds hardware well, making it reliable for cabinet construction.
Tea Chests & Boxes
Historically used for tea chests and storage boxes — its light weight combined with good strength makes it ideal for box and chest construction.
