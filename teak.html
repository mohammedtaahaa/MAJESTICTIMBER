About This Wood

The Story of Teak

Teak (Tectona grandis) is a tropical hardwood native to South and Southeast Asia, thriving in the monsoon forests of India, Myanmar, Thailand, and Indonesia. It has been prized for thousands of years — ancient Indian temples, royal palaces, and traditional furniture all showcase the enduring beauty of teak.

What makes teak truly exceptional is its unique combination of strength, natural oil content, and workability. The oils within teak wood act as a natural preservative, repelling water, preventing rot, and even resisting the damaging effects of harsh sunlight.

In Tamil Nadu and across South India, teak has long been the preferred choice for doors, windows, roof beams, and furniture. At Majestic Timber Depot, we source only the finest grade teak logs, ensuring every piece you receive is of the highest quality.

Did You Know?

Teak contains natural silica crystals that make it self-sharpening resistant — it was historically used to build warships and is still the preferred wood for luxury yacht decking worldwide.
