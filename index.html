Our Products

Premium Wood Varieties

We stock a curated selection of the finest hardwoods — available as raw logs or precision-cut sizes.

Teak
Tectona grandis

The king of hardwoods. Indian teak is prized worldwide for its exceptional durability, natural oil content, and gorgeous golden-brown grain. Highly resistant to moisture, insects, and weathering — perfect for furniture, flooring, and construction.

Furniture Flooring Construction Doors & Windows
Kapur Wood
Dryobalanops spp.

A highly valued timber from Southeast Asia, Kapur is known for its excellent strength-to-weight ratio and fine, even texture. Its pale yellowish-brown tone and smooth grain make it ideal for general construction, furniture, plywood, and interior joinery. Naturally durable and easy to work with.

Furniture Construction Plywood Interior Joinery
American Sal
Shorea robusta (American Grade)

A versatile and reliable hardwood known for its excellent load-bearing strength. American Sal is widely used in heavy construction, railway sleepers, and structural applications. Its dense grain makes it resistant to splitting and wear.

Structural Construction Beams Industrial
Mahogany
Swietenia mahagoni

A timeless classic in fine woodworking. Mahogany's rich reddish-brown tones, straight grain, and workability make it the top choice for premium furniture, cabinetry, and interior panelling. It finishes beautifully and holds polish exceptionally well.

Fine Furniture Cabinetry Panelling Interior Design
