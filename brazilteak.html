About This Wood

The Story of Brazil Teak

Brazil Teak, botanically known as Cumaru (Dipteryx odorata), is a premier hardwood from the tropical forests of South America, primarily Brazil, Venezuela, and Peru. Despite not being a true teak, it shares many of teak's most valued properties — and in several respects surpasses it.

Cumaru is one of the densest and hardest woods commercially available worldwide. Its extraordinarily tight grain structure and natural oil content give it outstanding resistance to wear, weathering, and biological attack. This makes it the benchmark material for premium outdoor decking, heavy-duty flooring, and structural applications demanding extreme durability.

At Majestic Timber Depot, we are proud to offer Brazil Teak as part of our premium and imported timber range. It is an exceptional choice for customers who demand the absolute best in durability, beauty, and long-term performance.

Did You Know?

Brazil Teak (Cumaru) is so hard and dense that it actually sinks in water — a testament to its extraordinary density of over 1,000 kg/m3. It is widely used as a sustainable alternative to endangered tropical hardwoods on international construction projects.

Applications

Best Uses of Brazil Teak

Premium Flooring
The world benchmark for hardwood flooring — Brazil Teak's extreme hardness means it withstands decades of heavy foot traffic without scratching or denting.
Outdoor Decking
One of the finest decking timbers available — its natural oils and extreme density make it impervious to rain, UV, and temperature extremes over many decades.
Heavy Construction
Structural beams, columns, bridges, and industrial flooring — Brazil Teak handles extreme loads and environmental stress with ease and reliability.
Marine Applications
Boat decking, dock construction, and marine piling — its resistance to moisture and marine borers makes it exceptional for water-contact applications.
Railway Sleepers
Extreme hardness and durability make Brazil Teak ideal for railway sleepers and high-vibration structural applications requiring lasting performance.
Luxury Furniture
High-end outdoor furniture, garden sets, and statement interior pieces — Brazil Teak's beauty and durability create furniture that lasts generations.
