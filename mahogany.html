About This Wood

The Story of Mahogany

Mahogany (Swietenia mahagoni) is one of the most celebrated and sought-after hardwoods in the world. Native to Central America and the Caribbean, it has been treasured by craftsmen, furniture makers, and architects for over 400 years.

What sets mahogany apart is its extraordinary combination of beauty and workability. Its straight, fine grain and uniform texture allow woodworkers to achieve incredibly precise cuts and perfect finishes — whether by hand or machine. It accepts stains uniformly and polishes to an unmatched sheen.

At Majestic Timber Depot, we supply premium mahogany for discerning buyers who demand the finest for their furniture, interior design, and cabinetry projects. When you choose mahogany, you're choosing a wood with a legacy of excellence.

Did You Know?

Mahogany became famous in 18th-century England when master craftsmen like Thomas Chippendale and Thomas Sheraton used it exclusively for their iconic furniture designs — which are now considered priceless antiques.
